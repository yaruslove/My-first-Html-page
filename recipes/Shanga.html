Shanga from Vyatka!

Every time when I visit my grand-mother in Kirov, I was treated to various dishes

One of favorite dishes in Kirovskaya-Oblast in Shnaga

Shanga is open "cake" with grated potato

Ingredients

  • Flour top grade
  • Vegetabels: potato, onion
  • cow oil

Recipe instructions

  1. Put grated potato and onion on top of a bun made of dough
  2. Bake in traditional Russian oven
  3. Lubricate with oil after oven
